The Gelfand Transform

Let AA\mathcal{A} be a Banach algebra over CC\mathbb{C}. Let △△\bigtriangleup be the space of all multiplicative linear functionals in AA\mathcal{A}, endowed with the weak-* topology. Let C⁢△C△C(\bigtriangleup) denote the algebra of complex valued continuous functions in △△\bigtriangleup.

The Gelfand transform is the mapping

^:A⟶C⁢△:^absent⟶AC△\widehat{}\;\;:\mathcal{A}\longrightarrow C(\bigtriangleup)
x⟼x^⟼x^xx\longmapsto\widehat{x}

where x^∈C⁢△^xC△\widehat{x}\in C(\bigtriangleup) is defined by x^(ϕ):=ϕ(x),∀ϕ∈△^x(ϕ)assignϕ(x),for-allϕ△\;\;\widehat{x}(\phi):=\phi(x),\;\;\;\forall\phi\in\bigtriangleup

The Gelfand transform is a continuous homomorphism from AA\mathcal{A} to C⁢△C△C(\bigtriangleup).

Theorem - Let C0⁢△subscriptC0△C_{{0}}(\bigtriangleup) denote the algebra of complex valued continuous functions in △△\bigtriangleup, that vanish at infinity. The image of the Gelfand transform is contained in C0⁢△subscriptC0△C_{{0}}(\bigtriangleup).

The Gelfand transform is a very useful tool in the study of commutative Banach algebras and, particularly, commutative $C^*$-algebras.

Classification of commutative C*superscriptCC^{*}-algebras: Gelfand-Naimark theorems

The following results are called the Gelfand-Naimark theorems. They classify all commutative C*superscriptCC^{*}-algebras and all commutative C*superscriptCC^{*}-algebras with identity element.

Theorem 1 - Let AA\mathcal{A} be a C*superscriptCC^{*}-algebra over CC\mathbb{C}. Then AA\mathcal{A} is *-isomorphic to C0⁢XsubscriptC0XC_{{0}}(X) for some locally compact Hausdorff space XXX. Moreover, the Gelfand transform is a *-isomorphism between AA\mathcal{A} and C0⁢△subscriptC0△C_{{0}}(\bigtriangleup).

Theorem 2 - Let AA\mathcal{A} be a unital C*superscriptCC^{*}-algebra over CC\mathbb{C}. Then AA\mathcal{A} is *-isomorphic to C⁢XCXC(X) for some compact Hausdorff space XXX. Moreover, the Gelfand transform is a *-isomorphism between AA\mathcal{A} and C⁢△C△C(\bigtriangleup).

The above theorems can be substantially improved. In fact, there is an equivalence between the category of commutative C*superscriptCC^{*}-algebras and the category of locally compact Hausdorff spaces. For more information and details about this, see the entry about the general Gelfand-Naimark theorem.
